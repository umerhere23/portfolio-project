About Dr. Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a highly skilled cardiologist with extensive experience in diagnosing and treating a wide range of cardiovascular conditions. She completed her residency at Massachusetts General Hospital and fellowship in Interventional Cardiology at Johns Hopkins Hospital.

Dr. Johnson is passionate about preventive cardiology and believes in a patient-centered approach to care. She takes the time to listen to her patients and develop personalized treatment plans that address their unique needs and concerns.
